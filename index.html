What We Handle

Residential Permitting Services

Every permit path is different. We navigate all of them — from simple additions to complex ADUs and multi-phase residential projects.

01 🏠

New Home Construction

Full permit coordination for single-family residential new builds from plan submission through final inspection.

02 🏗️

Room Additions & Remodels

Seamless permit handling for expansions, garage conversions, kitchen and bath remodels, and structural modifications.

03 🏡

ADU / JADU Permits

Accessory and Junior Accessory Dwelling Units have unique California code requirements — we know them inside out.

04 📐

Plan Check Management

We submit, track, respond to corrections, and resubmit until your plans are stamped and approved.

05 🔍

Inspection Coordination

Scheduling, attending, and resolving inspection items to keep your project moving without delay.

06 📄

Title 24 & Code Compliance

Energy compliance reports, accessibility requirements, and local code review — handled before problems arise.

How It Works

Four Steps to Your Permit.

01

Free Consultation

Tell us about your project. We assess scope, timelines, agency requirements, and what you need to get started — at no charge.

02

Document & Plan Review

We audit your plans for code compliance, flag corrections before submission, and prepare a complete, airtight permit package.

03

Agency Submission & Tracking

We submit to the building department, fire, planning, and any other required agencies — then actively track status and respond to every comment.

04

Permit Issued. You Build.

Once the permit is in your hands, we stay available through inspections and final sign-off. No surprise last steps.
